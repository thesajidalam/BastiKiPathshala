About Basti Ki Pathshala Foundation

Founded in 2015, Basti Ki Pathshala Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing quality education to underprivileged children in urban slums and rural areas.

We believe that every child deserves access to education, regardless of their socio-economic background. Our team of passionate educators and volunteers work tirelessly to bridge the educational gap in underserved communities.
